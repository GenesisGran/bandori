Sakuragawa Megu (櫻川めぐ) was born on October 24, 1988 in Sakuragawa City, Ibaraki, Japan. She is the voice actress of Udagawa Ako and Roselia's drummer. She is currently affiliated with S-Inc, while her singing activities are signed under Lantis.
Her former stage name was 桜川めぐ (same name and reading but with different kanji).
