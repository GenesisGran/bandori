Terakawa Aimi (寺川愛美), professionally known by the mononym of Aimi (愛美), was born on December 25, 1991 in Kobe City, Hyogo Prefecture, Japan. She is the voice actress of Toyama Kasumi and vocalist and rhythm guitarist of Poppin'Party. She is affiliated with HiBiKi and her musical activities are signed under King Records.
